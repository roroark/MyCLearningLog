On Executing files...
Refering to http://stackoverflow.com/questions/19628460/how-do-i-run-c-program-without-using
Every local executable executed with ./
To remove ./ before execution, add it to the 'Environment Variable' 'PATH'
PATH specifies the list of directories where executables are located. More: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Environment_variable
To edit PATH, follow these: http://hathaway.cc/post/69201163472/how-to-edit-your-path-environment-variables-on-mac
Remember to follow up with source ~/.bash_profile
